"Hatching Eggs Techniques"

To hatch a pokemon egg you would have to put them in a incubator and walk certain distance to hatch them. but make sure you are walking, biking, driving under 12mph for the game to accept it.

  • 2km egg = 1.2 miles
  • 6km egg = 3 miles
  • 10km egg = 6 miles

"Throwing Pokeballs"

Throwing pokeballs will help catch pokemon to accumalate more exp in the you throw them a certain way.

  • Pokeballs (Acquire Lvl 1)
  • Great Balls (Acquire Lvl 12)
  • Ultra Balls (Acquire Lvl 20)
